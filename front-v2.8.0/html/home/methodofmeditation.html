Method of Meditation

Guru Siyag has simplified the process of achieving good health and enlightenment by devising a simple & easy-to-follow method of meditation.

  • 1.

    Sit in a comfortable position facing any direction.You can sit cross legged on the floor or on a chair or lie down in case unable to sit.

  • 2.

    Look at Gurudev’s image for a minute or two.

  • 3.

    Then close your eyes and try to see Gurudev’s image at the centre of your forehead and silently pray to Gurudev to help you meditate for 15 minutes.

  • 4.

    Mentally chant the Sanjeevani mantra given by Gurudev ( without moving your lips and tongue ) while meditating.

  • 5.

    During this period, if you undergo any type of yogic exercises, do not panic. Let it happen. Do not try to stop them.These yogic postures are a part of your inner cleansing.These will stop once the requested time period of meditation is over.

  • 6.

    Meditate in this way for 15 minutes in the morning and evening on an empty stomach.r
