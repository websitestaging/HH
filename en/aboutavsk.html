About AVSK

Gurudev Siyag had institutionalized his Spiritual Mission by setting up Adhyatma Vigyan Satsang Kendra (AVSK), Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India – A purely spiritual organization, registered with Devsthan Department under Rajasthan Government.

Contact AVSK
The organization is dedicated to revival, promotion and propagation of India’s Spiritual Science to bring about Spiritual Transformation in mankind.

Aims of AVSK:

  • Spread this divine spiritual knowledge for the benefit of mankind irrespective of their caste, colour, creed, sex, religion or nationality and establish center’s of Adhyatma Vigyan Satsang Kendra in the entire world.

  • To free the mankind from the distortions and hypocrisies prevalent in every religion of the world; To realize and experience the Supreme Power within one’s own heart according to the principles of Siddha Yoga

  • For a better world, transform the people from within by Siddha Yoga so that they can spread the divine light around them.

  • To awaken the consciousness of all the positive minded people of the world by Shaktipat Initiation so that they are instrumental in spreading this knowledge to establish World Peace.

  • To transform mankind by Shaktipat Initiation as described in Siddha Yoga Philosophy from Tamo Guna to Rajo Guna; Rajo Guna to Sattva Guna; and Sattva Guna to TRIGUNNATTIT.

Anyone who has been initiated into Siddha Yoga by Gurudev Siyag is considered to be an AVSK member. No formal registration or payment of fee is required for a disciple to be an AVSK member.

  • Active AVSK members work as volunteers to carry out various duties to serve the mission. The duties include canvassing through wall-posters and public contacts; organizing Gurudev Siyag’s initiation programs; make audio-visual presentations on Gurudev’s mission; propagating Gurudev’s mission via social media and conducting meditation sessions in India and abroad.

  • AVSK, being a non-profit spiritual, organization, has no source of income of its own nor does it seek any funding from Government or Corporate houses.

  • All its activities are funded through voluntary contributions in cash or kind by Gurudev Siyag’s disciples.

  • AVSK volunteers can set up self-funded meditation centers in the cities they live after due consent from Ashram Headquarters.
