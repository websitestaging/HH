This Whole World Is A Vast Image Of Only One Supreme Power

bijam tad aham arjuna

bhutam caracaram

Lord Krishna (BG 10:39)

— Lord Krishna has said in the 39th Shlok (Verse) of Chapter 10 in ‘Geeta’ (An Indian Scripture)

  • Transalation: Furthermore O’ Arjuna, I am the generating seeds of all existences. There is no being-moving or non-moving- that can exist without me.

Athava bahunaitena
kim jnatena tavarjuna
Vistabhyahamidam krtsnam
ekamsena sthito jagat

Lord Krishna (BG 10:42)

— Lord Krishna has said in the 42nd Shlok (verse) of Chapter 10 in ‘Geeta’ (An Indian Scripture)

  • Transalation: But what need is there, Arjuna, for all this detailed knowledge? With a single fragment of Myself I pervade and support this entire universe.

  • In this situation, it is meaningless to blame anyone for whatever is happening in the world. The Supreme Power by deluding all beings, is guiding everything according to HIS will. In this relation the instruction given by Bhagawan Shri Krishna to Maharishi Aurobindo in Alipore jail clearly shows that Bhagawan has certainly descended in India.

  • Bhagawan Shri Krishna said to Maharishi Aurobindo,” I am raising this country to spread my message. This message is the message of that eternal religion (Sanatan Dharma) about which you didn’t know so far but you know it now. When you go out of the jail tell your countrymen that you are rising for the eternal religion (Sanatan Dharma). You are being raised for the world and not to fulfil your selfish motives.”

The churches will lose their existence by the end of 20th century.
It is evident that the Supreme Power by descending in India, wants to bring a transformation (yugparivartan) by re-establishing the Sanatan Dharma (Eternal religion) as the only religion in the world.

  • From the above instruction given to Maharishi Aurobindo, it is evident that the Supreme Power by descending in India, wants to bring a transformation (yugparivartan) by re-establishing the Sanatan Dharma (Eternal religion) as the only religion in the world. It has been foretold by the Christian Saints,” The churches will lose their existence by the end of 20th century.”

I have to tell you many more things but you cannot bear them now. When that ‘Soul of Truth’ will come, he will guide you towards the complete truth. He will not say anything from his side. He will say only what he will hear.

Jesus

— Jesus had told his disciples

  • In his last days, Jesus had told his disciples-“I am telling you the truth. It is beneficial for you that I go leaving you behind. If I don’t go then the one who will be helping you, will not come. But if I go, I will send him to you. When he will come, he will declare the world guilty in relation to sins, religiousness and justice. Sins’ because the people of the world didn’t trust me.‘Religiousness’ because I am going back to Father and you will not be able to see me again.‘Justice’ because the ruler of the world has been proved guilty. I have to tell you many more things but you cannot bear them now. When that ‘Soul of Truth’ will come, he will guide you towards the complete truth. He will not say anything from his side. He will say only what he will hear. He will tell you about the forthcoming events. He will glorify me because he will understand what I said and will tell you. What is Father’s, is mine, that is why I said thatTHAT soul will understand what I said and will tell you.”

  • These words,” What is father’s is mine” clearly shows that the Power which will descend will have direct connection with the Supreme Power because apart from the Supreme Power no one else can know the future.

mrtyuh sarva-haras caham

smrtir medha dhrtih ksama

Lord Krishna (BG 10:34)

— Lord Krishna has said in the 34th Shlok(verse) of Chapter 10 in ‘Geeta’ (An Indian Scripture)

  • I am all-devouring death, and I am the generating principle of all that is yet to be. Among women I am fame, fortune, fine speech, memory, intelligence, steadfastness and patience.>

  • In the above verse, Bhagawan Shri Krishna has said that he is the cause of all that is yet to be born in the world. The saying by Jesus that that ‘Soul of Truth’,” will tell you the events of the future” clarifies that THAT Supreme Power is going to descend in the form of a human. Similarly, many saints of the world have been foretelling about the descent of the Supreme Power for a long time.

  • In the 15th century, a British lady had declared that,” In the last two decades of the 20th century, entire world will be in the grip of civil war and will bear huge loss of mankind and wealth. In the last decade of the 20th century, a man will appear from the northern part of India who will address the world. Whatever he will say, will be proved true and the whole world will start following him.”

This way peace and happiness will be established in the world in the 21st century and India will regain its title of ‘Jagatguru’ (Spiritual Guru of the World).
So, the prediction by Maharishi Aurobindo about the descent of Bhagawan Shri Krishna on 24th Nov’1926 will be proved true.
