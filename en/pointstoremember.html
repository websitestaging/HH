Points to Remember

Some points to keep in mind.

  • For Shaktipat Initiation, it is compulsory to first hear the Sanjeevani Mantra in Gurudev’s divine voice. Without hearing it in His voice, the mantra has no effect.

  • Chanting is the key. Gurudev said to chant the mantra round- the- clock while doing all the daily activities like bathing, driving, eating etc.

  • Chanting is to be done without moving the lips and tongue.

  • Meditation has to be done preferably on an empty stomach.

  • Focus on Gurudev’s image at the center of the forehead (Third Eye spot) while meditating and continue mental chanting of the Sanjeevani mantra during meditation too.

  • When following GSSY practice, only ‘Sanjeevani’ Mantra has to be chanted. No other mantra to be chanted along with it.

  • Mantra has to be first heard in Gurudev Shri Ramlal ji Siyag’s voice to get Shaktipat initiation and also for it to be effective.

  • Meditate only for 15 minutes and not more than that.

  • You don’t need to give up your religion. Just follow the GSSY practice as told by Gurudev.

  • No need to change your life style or give up anything as Gurudev always said, “You need not give up the things, the things will give you up.
