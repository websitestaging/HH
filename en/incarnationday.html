Gurudev Siyag's Incarnation Day

Gurudev Shri Ramlalji Siyag was born on Nov 24, 1926 in Palana Village of Bikaner district, Rajasthan, India.

Gurudev Shri Ramlalji Siyag has attained two Siddhis - ‘Gayatri Siddhi’ (God in formless state) on Jan 1, 1969 & ‘Krishna Siddhi’ (God in form) in 1984.

Gurudev chanted the Gayatri Mantra from the October of 1968 and attained the ‘Gayatri Siddhi’ (God in formless state) on Jan 1, 1969. After reading some books by Swami Vivekanand, Gurudev accepted Baba Shri Gangainath Ji as his Guru and by His grace Gurudev attained ‘Krishna Siddhi’ (God in form) in 1984.

Because of attaining these two Siddhis, Gurudev is empowered to awaken the Kundalini through Shaktipat Dikhsa (initiation). By the order of his Spiritual Master Baba Shri Gangainath ji, Gurudev has been giving Shaktipat initiation since 1990 for the welfare of mankind. Since then he has awakened the Kundalini of lakhs of disciples by Shaktipat initiation as described in Siddha Yoga.

He established Adhyatm Vigyan Satsang Kendra, Jodhpur in 1993 to spread Siddha Yoga philosophy in the world for the welfare of mankind

  • A grand programme is organized at AVSK, Jodhpur every year on 24th November to celebrate the appearance day of Gurudev.

  • Thousands of disciples from all over India and abroad gather at AVSK, Jodhpur to pay their respects to Gurudev and seek his blessings.

  • After listening to the speech by Gurudev, all the disciples meditate together. This is followed by a grand feast.

  • This day is also celebrated at various centers of AVSK with great enthusiasm and reverence.
